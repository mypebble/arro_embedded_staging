Run campaign

Use this screen to choose a campaign to promote your project community page.

Running a campaign helps you to spread the word about your project and gain interest from people who want to help you and see you succeed. Market research indicates that it takes 6 - 8 contact points to generate a return. The campaigns Pebble provides are designed to take you up to the minimum number of contact points with your stakeholders to encourage them to sign up to help you.

Note: This is important. It might seem that sending out five emails over the course of five weeks is a lot but market research shows that this is not enough. You will also need to share the project on social media.

Pebble provides a number of different types of campaign for you to choose from. Once you have chosen the campaign you want to run, the campaign is easy to create because you are led through the process step-by-step.

Create a campaign?

Campaigns are straightforward to create and you are led through the steps to create the campaign once you choose the campaign you want to run.

  1. Click Select Campaign.
  2. Select the Project you want to run the campaign for.

    3. Note: This step is required if you are creating a campaign from the Campaigns module. If you are creating a campaign after publishing your project community page, this step is done for you automatically.

  3. Choose your campaign Audience (stakeholders with email addresses are shown), and click Next.
  4. Preview the campaign by reviewing and amending the campaign text for each email that will be sent.
    • Click Click to preview and edit alongside the email.
      • To view the text, scroll down the pop-up box that appears.
      • To edit the text, click Edit, amend the text in the message and click Done.
    • Click to close the Preview window.
    • Click Next.
  5. Choose the frequency of sending out emails and the date that you want to send the first email. After that date, the frequency determines the send dates for the subsequent emails.

  6. Click Send.
