Stakeholders

Use this screen to manage your stakeholderA person or organisation who has an interest in your projects. relationships, including if they are eligible for Gift AidThis is a UK government scheme which lets registered charities reclaim a percentage of tax on donations from UK taxpayers. and are interested in volunteering time to help your school projects. The more stakeholders you have, the more specifically you can target campaigns and the further your reach in the wider community in terms of getting your message out about school projects.

How do I ...

Add new stakeholders?

You can add stakeholders manually one at a time, or by importing a batch of stakeholders from a file.

Add a batch of stakeholders from a file
  • Click + Add New > Import Data and follow the instructions on the help for the import data wizard.
Add the following details for stakeholders you are adding manually
  • Click +Add New > Create Manually and add the details for the stakeholder as follows:
    • Name

    The stakeholder name. If you click the name this opens a window with the stakeholder details for you to amend.

    Type

    The stakeholder can be:

    • Pupil
    • Family
    • AlumniThe term used to describe former pupils at a school.
    • Staff
    • Person
    • Business
    • Community Organisation
    • Charity
    • Person
    • Organisation
    • User
    • Donor

    The profile type gives an indication of whether they are eligible for Gift Aid or to be a volunteer for your school. For example, a pupil will not be eligible for Gift Aid, as Gift Aid donors are UK taxpayers. However you may link to the Pupil for your records but enter the parent’s name as the Gift Aid donor.

    An alumni or person profile type may or may not be eligible for Gift Aid. This depends on whether or not they are retired and if they are, whether they still pay tax. For example, if a retired person has savings and pays Income or Capital Gains Tax of an equal or higher amount than that being claimed (25p in the pound), then they are eligible for Gift Aid.

    Contact Details

    The stakeholder contact details. Hover the cursor over the icons to see the details. For example, hover the cursor over the home to display the postal address, over the letter to display the email address, the telephone to display the telephone number

    Gift Aid

    If the stakeholder is eligible for Gift Aid, the Gift Aid icon is shown in green. If not, it is shown in grey.

    Volunteer

    If the stakeholder has indicated they want to help by volunteering, the Volunteer icon is shown in yellow. If not, it is shown in grey.

Amend stakeholder details?
  1. Find the stakeholder in the list of stakeholders.
  2. Click the stakeholder name to display the stakeholder details.
  3. Click Edit.
  4. Amend the details required.

    5. For example, if the stakeholder was eligible for Gift Aid but isn’t any longer, clear the This contact is eligible for Gift Aid checkbox.

  5. Click Save.
Find a stakeholder?
  • Filter the list of names displayed in the stakeholder list by entering the name or characters from the stakeholder name in the Search box.
Mark a stakeholder out of use?
  1. Find the stakeholder in the list of stakeholders.
  2. Select the check box to the left of the stakeholder name.
  3. Select Out of Use > Mark Out of Use.
Hide stakeholders that are not in use?

Note: You can only hide stakeholders that are not in use, if you have marked stakeholders not in use previously.

  • Click Hide Out of Use.
Show stakeholders that are not in use?

You can only show stakeholders that are not in use, if you have previously marked stakeholders not in use and hidden them previously.

  • Click Show Out of Use.
Mark a stakeholder in use?

By default, stakeholders are created as in use, so you don’t need to mark a stakeholder as in use unless you have marked the stakeholder as out of use previously.

  1. If the toggle button Show Out of Use / Hide Out of Use is displayed with the text Show Out of Use, then click Show Out of Use to display stakeholders that have been marked out of use previously.
  2. Find the stakeholder in the list of stakeholders.
  3. Select the check box to the left of the stakeholder name.
  4. Select Out of Use > Mark Back in Use.
Export stakeholder list?
  • Click Export Data. The stakeholders information is downloaded to your PC as a .xls file.
