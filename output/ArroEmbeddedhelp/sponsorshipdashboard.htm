Sponsorship Dashboard

Sponsors can be a mainstay of project support. Partnering with businesses is a mutually beneficial endeavour – your school receives support for your projects (monetary or otherwise) and your business partner will reap rewards as well, such as fostering positive relations with their future workforce, promotional opportunities to members of your school community and fulfilling their corporate social responsibilities. Use this screen to build and manage your relationships with your sponsors and prospective major donors.

How do I ...

Add sponsor leads?

Provided you have published your project community page, people can record their interest in sponsoring the project through the sponsorship form on the project community page. You can also add new leads manually.

  1. Click +Add direct enquiry.
  2. Enter the Name, Company, Contact Number and Email.
  3. Click Add Lead.
  4. If you have enough information to qualify the lead, you can do this now See Qualify leads.
Collect required information from leads?

To qualify a sponsor lead, you need to know:

  • The project they are interested in sponsoring. Email the volunteer to ask for this, if they haven’t already specified a project. You can and should send them details of all projects they are interested in sponsoring.
  • The amount of money the sponsor is willing to pledge. Email the volunteer to ask for this, if they haven’t already specified this.

Note: Paperwork and agreements for pledges are handled offline.

Add notes to sponsors?

At any time you can add notes for a sponsor lead. This means you can record the history of the contact you have with the sponsor.

  1. Click the sponsor card.
  2. Enter notes in the Add a new note box.
  3. Click Post.
Qualify leads?

You can qualify leads when you have contacted and discussed with the sponsor the project(s) they are interested in sponsoring and the amount they are able to pledge.

  1. Click the card in the New Leads column.
  2. Select Sent Details.
  3. Select Contacted.
  4. Select the project the lead is going to sponsor from the To Sponsor drop-down list.
  5. Select the amount the lead is going to pledge from the To Pledge drop-down list.
  6. Click Qualify Lead.
Confirm pledges?

Paperwork and agreements for pledges are handled offline. Provided you have the agreed paperwork to support the confirmation, you can confirm pledges.

  1. Click the card in the Qualified Leads column.
  2. Check the To Sponsor selection.
  3. Check the To Pledge amount.
  4. If an initial donation has been given, enter the received donation amount.

    5. Note: This donation cannot be Gift Aided.

  5. Click Confirm Pledge.
Add donations?

You can add donations to leads which have been pledged, as and when the money arrives from the sponsor. The sponsor card moves to the Completed column once the total amount pledged has been recorded.

  1. Click the card in the Pledged column.

    2. The amount that has been pledged and the amount that has been donated to date are displayed along with the percentage complete value, so you can see at a glance how the sponsorship pledge is doing.

  2. Enter the received donation amount.
  3. Click Add Donation.
Reduce or correct a donation amount?

You can correct or reduce donation amounts that were entered incorrectly by clicking the card in the Pledged column and entering a negative value in the Add received donation amount box and clicking Add Donation.
