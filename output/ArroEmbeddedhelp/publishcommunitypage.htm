Publish community page

Use this screen to begin sharing your project community page through a campaign or through social media or your school website.

Note: Promoting your project is very important. The sooner you begin to promote the project, the more donations will flow in.

How do I ...

Create a campaign?
  • Click Create a Campaign.

    • Follow the instructions on the next screen to create your campaign.

Share the community page on Twitter
  • Click .

    • In the Twitter window which appears, text which will promote your project is displayed.

    For example, the following is displayed for the project Burn While You Learn!.

    ,

    • Authorising Pebble to tweet for you?

      You are asked to authorise Pebble to post tweets on your behalf. You only need to do this once.

      • If you are already logged in to Twitter:
        • Click Authorize App.
      • If you are not already logged in to Twitter:
        • Enter your school Twitter Username or email.
        • Enter your school Twitter Password.
        • Click Sign In.

      Authorisation is recorded within your school Twitter account and you are directed back to Arro which is redisplayed.

    • Enter the text you want to tweet and click Tweet.

      • Pebble posts the text as a tweet on the school twitter feed for you and a success message is shown.

Note: If you are new to Twitter, click Sign up to create an account so that you can promote your project by tweeting about it. If you don’t know anything about Twitter or tweeting, please refer to Twitter’s help on Getting started with Twitter.

Share the community page on Facebook
  • Click
  • In the Facebook window that appears, log in to your Facebook account to share.

Note: If you are new to Faceboook, click Create New Account to create an account so that you can promote your project by sharing it on Facebook. If you don’t know anything about Facebook, please refer to Facebook’s help which you can access here.
