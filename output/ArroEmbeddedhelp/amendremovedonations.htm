Amend or Remove Donations

Use this screen to amend or remove donations that you have already recorded.

Donations are not the only way that schools can accrue extra funding these days but in 2017 a survey has found that over a third of parents are now asked to donate to schools.

Maximising income from your donations is important and one way to do this is by claiming gift aid for all donations which qualify. It’s your responsibility to ask the donor to confirm they are eligible for Gift Aid. You can do this by downloading a declaration form which they can sign, or by asking them to confirm online via email, for example. You can check HMRC requirements for claiming gift aid here

If a donor, who has been eligible for Gift Aid retires, then you need to amend the Gift Aid status for the donor. Changing gift aid status is just one reason you might need to amend the details for a donation. Other reasons might be changes of donor contact details, or payment type. Removing donations is something you only need to do if you have made an error in adding details of a donation.

You can only amend or remove donations which have not yet been submitted in a claim to HMRC and when the period the donation was made has not yet been closed in Fund Manager. However, if you have submitted a claim to HMRC before being notified the donor status has changed, you can declare this when you create the next claim for Gift Aid. The important thing to remember is to keep your stakeholder details up-to-date and this is something you need to do by editing the stakeholder details in the Campaigns module. This will ensure claims in future are made with the correct Gift Aid status.

Note: You can amend donations that you haven’t yet submitted to HMRC and when the period the donation was made in has not yet been closed in Fund ManagerOnce you remove a donation, you cannot reverse the action.

How do I ...

Amend a donation?
  1. Find the donation you want to amend and click the amend icon in the column at the right hand side of the screen.
  2. On the Amend Donation screen, amend any of the details you need to amend.

    3. For example if you need to change the Gift Aid status of the donor, either select or clear the check box for Claim Gift Aid on this donation.

    Note: Remember to change the status of the donor, do this through Campaigns by selecting the Stakeholders tab and then editing the details for the stakeholder.

  3. Click Amend Donation.
Remove a donation?
  1. Find the transaction you want to cancel and click the Remove icon in the column at the right hand side of the screen.
  2. On the Remove Donation screen, check the details of the transaction to ensure it is the one you want to remove.

    3. Warning! You cannot reverse this once you have removed the transaction so be sure it is the one you want to remove.

  3. When you are sure this is what you want to remove, click Remove Donation.
