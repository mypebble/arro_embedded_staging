Lettings

Lettings can be a source of income for your school projects. This dashboard is where you build and manage the portfolio of your school premises and items for hire. You can record and audit what you offer and market your portfolio either direct to third parties for a fee, or trading rooms and equipment availability with other schools in your locality. This lets you maximise your school real estate, making it earn you revenue and fill in gaps by working with other schools in order to offer more both to pupils and the wider community.

The first time you use this dashboard, it will display an introductory video which you can watch by clicking Play. Alternatively, you can click Get Started to begin creating your portfolio of lettings. After first time use, the dashboard displays the lettings you have chosen previously and their status.
Incomplete

The letting is in process but you have yet to finish it. You can edit the letting and you can preview what it will look like when it goes live.

Ready to publish

The letting is ready to publish. All the information that is needed to publish the letting has been included. You can edit the letting and you can preview what it will look like when it goes live.

Published

The letting has been published and is live on the hire.joinos.com site. You can edit the letting and you can view the live page that has been published.

Archived

The letting has been retired and is no longer live on the hire.joinos.com site. You can edit the letting and preview what it will look like if you publish it again.

How do I ...

Build a portfolio of lettings?
  • You can choose to add a new letting by choosing a template from a library of letting templates and adjusting the template text to match your unique offering.
  • You can add a new letting from scratch, entering all the details yourself.
Add a letting from the letting templates?
  1. Open . .
  2. Choose the template to base your letting on.
  3. Edit the details of the template to match your own offering.
    Name A short name which helps you quickly identify the facility.
    Summary A short description which expands on the Name to cover key features of the facility.
    Contact The name of the contact for the facility.
    Contact Phone Number The phone number of the contact.
    Description An expansion of the Summary.
    Key Features The key selling points for the facility.
    Details An expansion of the Description, giving full details of what is offered, and the terms and conditions of the hire.
    Availability Dates and times the facility is available.
    Prices Rates of hiring, deposit and cancellation costs.
    Testimonial Endorsement for the facility.
    Endorser Who has endorsed the facility.
  4. Adding images
    1. Click + Add Image to add an image.
    2. Click Choose a file.
    3. Select the image to upload and click Open.
    4. Click Upload.

      5. Tip: If you upload an image you didn’t mean to, select the image. click Delete and confirm the deletion when prompted.

    5. If you upload more than one image, select which image is the primary image.

      6. Note: If you upload only one image this is the primary image by default.

  5. To save the details:
    • To save the details and publish later, click Save and publish later.
    • To publish now, click Publish now.

      • Note: To publish, you must add a contact, a contact phone number and at least one image.

Add a letting from scratch?
  1. Open . .
  2. Select Lettings.
  3. Click +New Letting and then follow the instructions for adding a new letting.
Amend a letting?
  1. Open . .
  2. Select Lettings.
  3. Click Edit on the item template to base your letting on.
  4. Amend the details as required.
    Name A short name which helps you quickly identify the facility.
    Summary A short description which expands on the Name to cover key features of the facility.
    Contact The name of the contact for the facility.
    Contact Phone Number The phone number of the contact.
    Description An expansion of the Summary.
    Key Features The key selling points for the facility.
    Details An expansion of the Description, giving full details of what is offered, and the terms and conditions of the hire.
    Availability Dates and times the facility is available.
    Prices Rates of hiring, deposit and cancellation costs.
    Testimonial Endorsement for the facility.
    Endorser Who has endorsed the facility.
  5. Amend images
    1. To add an image, click + Add Image.
      1. Click Choose a file.
      2. Select the image to upload and click Open.
      3. Click Upload.
    2. To delete an image, select the image, click Delete and confirm the deletion when prompted.
    3. To select an image as the primary image, select the image and click Set As Primary.

      4. Note: If you upload only one image this is the primary image by default.

  6. To save the details:
    • If you have not previously published the page, and don’t want to publish it yet, click Save and publish later.
    • If you have not previously published the page, and you do want to publish it now, click Publish now.
    • If you have previously published the page, and you want to publish the changes, click Publish Changes.

      • Note: To publish, you must add a contact, a contact phone number and at least one image.

Preview a letting?
  1. Open . .
  2. Select Lettings.
  3. Click Preview on the letting you want to preview.

    4. Because the preview offers a draft view of the published layout, the Book Online buttons are greyed out.

Publish a letting?
  1. Open . .
  2. Select Lettings.
  3. Click Edit on the letting you want to publish.
  4. To publish the letting:
    • Click Publish now if the letting has not been published before.
    • Click Publish changes if the letting has been published before.
View a live letting?
  1. Open . .
  2. Select Lettings.
  3. Click View Page on a Published letting.
Manage images for my letting?
  1. Open . .
  2. Select Lettings.
  3. Click Edit on the letting.
  4. To add an image, click + Add Image.
    1. Click Choose a file.
    2. Select the image to upload and click Open.
    3. Click Upload.
  5. To delete an image, select the image and click Delete.
  6. To choose the primary image for the letting, select the primary image and click Set As Primary.

    7. Note: If you upload only one image this is the primary image by default.

Archive a letting?
  1. Open . .
  2. Select Lettings.
  3. Click Edit on an Incomplete, Ready to Publish or Published letting.
  4. Click Archive.
Restore an archived letting?
  1. Open . .
  2. Select Lettings.
  3. Click Restore on an Archived letting.

    4. The letting restores with its previous status. For example, if it was published before being archived, it is restored as published.

Choose featured lettings for hire.joinos.com?
  1. Open . .
  2. Select Lettings.
  3. Click the items you want to select. The favourite icon turns fully yellow when selected.
  4. Click Done.
Promote my lettings?

You need to promote and share your lettings in order to make them work for the school. We offer different ways for you to do this.

  • Share your Services & Lettings hire.joinos.com site
  • Share links to individual lettings. Each letting has its own URL which you can share.
Share your Services & Lettings hire.joinos.com site?

  1. Open .

  2. Select Lettings.

    3. You will see the details of Your Services & Lettings Site at the top right hand of the screen:

    Note: You will only see this link once you have published at least oneservice or letting.

  3. To share your site URL:
    1. Click Visit Site.
    2. Highlight the URL in the bar at the top of the site.
    3. Right-click and select Copy from the menu that appears.

      4. You can now paste the URL into a separate document so you can include it in your school website, or within email campaigns.

  4. To share your site on Facebook, click Facebook, log in to Facebook and share a post that links to the site.
  5. To share your site on Twitter, click Twitter.
    • Authorising Pebble to tweet for you?

      You are asked to authorise Pebble to post tweets on your behalf. You only need to do this once.

      • If you are already logged in to Twitter:
        • Click Authorize App.
      • If you are not already logged in to Twitter:
        • Enter your school Twitter Username or email.
        • Enter your school Twitter Password.
        • Click Sign In.

      Authorisation is recorded within your school Twitter account and you are directed back to Arro which is redisplayed.

    • Enter the text you want to tweet and click Tweet.

      • Pebble posts the text as a tweet on the school twitter feed for you and a success message is shown.
