View donation

Use this screen to view donations that have been made. You can search for specific donations by donor name, type of payment or description. You might want to do this if you are checking to see if Gift Aid was claimed for the donation, or which donations were made in cash.

How do I ...

Find a donation?
  • Use the Search box at the top of the screen to search for specific donations.
    • To find a donation by donor name, enter the donor name in the Search box.
    • To find a donation by description, enter the description in the Search box.
    • To find a donation by payment type, enter the payment type in the Search box.
Check if a donation included Gift Aid?
  1. Locate the donation you want to check.
  2. Look at the Gift Aid column. If the icon is grey, Gift Aid wasnâ€™t included. If it is green, then it was.
  3. To find out how much Gift Aid was included, hover the mouse over the green Gift Aid icon and the amount shows above the icon.
Add a new donation?
  1. Click +Add New and follow the help instructions on the Record Donation screen.
