Volunteers Dashboard

Some people like to donate their time in addition to or instead of donating money. People who have a desire to help by giving their time are valuable assets to your income generation journey. Not only can they be called upon to perform labour-intensive tasks for your project, they are likely to be passionate about your project and are likely to spread the word, achieving a personal outreach that far exceeds traditional methods. Use this screen to build and manage your volunteer network.

How do I ...

Build a volunteer network?

You can build your volunteer network the following ways:

  • By running Campaign Messages.
  • Through your Joinos Community page where people can register an interest in volunteering to help you.
  • Word-of-mouth requests via other volunteers.
Create a volunteer campaign?

Click Create Volunteer Campaign and follow the instructions in the wizard.

Search for volunteers?
  • Use the Search box to find specific volunteers.
Add volunteer leads?

Provided you have published your project community page, people can record their interest in volunteering to help the project through the volunteer form on the project community page. You can also add volunteer leads manually.

  1. Click +Add New.
    • If you want to add a contact from Campaigns:
      1. Click +Contact.
      2. Search and select the Campaigns contact you want to add as a volunteer.

        3. Once you select the contact, the contact Name, Telephone Number and Email are automatically inserted into the Add Volunteer form.

    • If you want to add contact details manually:
      1. Enter the Name, Telephone Number and Email.
      2. Click Add Volunteer.
  2. If you have enough information to confirm the volunteer, you can do this now See Collect required information from leads and Confirm volunteer leads.
Collect required information from leads?

To confirm a volunteer lead, you need to know:

  • The project they are interested in helping with. Email the volunteer to ask for this, if they haven’t already specified this.
  • The amount of time they have available to help you. Email the volunteer to ask for this, if they haven’t already specified this.
  • That they have passed DBS (previously CRB) checks. Follow the instructions on the www.gov.uk website for this.
Confirm volunteer leads?

Once you have collected all required information from volunteer leads you can confirm the volunteer.

  1. Click the card in the Volunteer Leads column.
  2. Select the project the volunteer wants to help with from the To Volunteer on on drop-down list.
  3. Enter the estimated amount of hours the volunteer ha pledged.
  4. Select Contacted.
  5. Click Confirm.
Add notes to volunteers?

At any time you can add notes for a volunteer lead. This means you can record the history of the contact you have with the volunteer. For example you could add the date you received the DBS check approval. You can also add other information here, for example, if the volunteer is trained in first aid, when they qualified and last refresher date.

  1. Click the lead card in the Volunteer Leads column, or the volunteer Name in the list of Active Volunteers.
  2. Enter notes in the Add a new note box.
  3. Click Post.
Remove volunteers?

You can remove the volunteer by clearing the Volunteer Status check box for the contact in the Campaigns module.
