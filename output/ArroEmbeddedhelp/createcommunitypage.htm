Create and update community page

The project community page is the central focal point for advertising your project online and sharing information about the project and its progress. It is absolutely essential that you use this page to share news about your project’s progress and to appeal for support in the wider community for your project and its aims. Once you start sharing your project community page you will start to see returns in donations and people signing up to volunteer to help with the project or to provide sponsorship for the project.

Use this screen to view and make changes to your project’s community page and publish the changes.

How do I ...

Understand the community page elements?

The page has a number of elements which help people understand more about the project and what it will mean if they donate to it.

Note: The following is an example of a community page to help you recognise the different parts.

Header

The header contains the project image, title and the first of two Donate buttons. It also includes the current amount donated to the project along with the number of supporters.

Narrative

The narrative section of the web page contains the story behind the project, the key benefits the project will have and messages from the project’s supporters. Beneath the narrative are the second of two Donate buttons and Facebook and Twitter links for people to share the project story with a social network.

Enquiries about sponsoring a project

If a business organisation would like to sponsor the project by providing financial support, they can let you know by clicking on the Enquire Online button and filling in their details. When they do this, the details are recorded on the Stakeholders tab.

Volunteers for the project

If people are interested in helping the project by giving some of their time to help with the project, they can use the Become a Volunteer form to register their interest in doing this. When they do this, the details are recorded on the Stakeholders tab.

About the school

Some background information about the school.

Footer

The footer contains email addresses and telephone numbers which donors or sponsors may find useful.

Change the image?

Note: If you want to upload your own images to put on the community page, and you are not sure how to do this, send the images to Pebble and we will upload them for you. You will then be able to select them from the drop-down list of images.

  1. Click Edit.
  2. To change the image at the top of the community page, click Change Image.
  3. To select an image of your own, click +Custom.
  4. Select an image from the available images.
  5. Click I’m Done Editing.
Change the Logo?
  • Send the image of the school logo to us at Pebble and we will make the change on your community page.
Change the theme?
  1. Click Edit.
  2. Click Theme.
  3. Select the theme that best matches your school’s theme.
  4. Click I’m Done Editing.
Change the Project Name?
  1. Click Edit.
  2. Click in the Name to place the cursor in the title box, and then make the amendments you want.
  3. Click I’m Done Editing.
Change the Project Story?
  1. Click Edit.
  2. Click in the Our Project Story box to place the cursor in the box, and then make the amendments you want.
  3. Click I’m Done Editing.
Add a link to a web page?
  1. Click Edit.
  2. Click in the Our Project Story box and place the cursor where you want to add the link.
  3. Add the link to the web page that relates to your project by wrapping your link in square brackets and inserting the link in normal brackets immediately afterwards. For example,

    4. [Click on this link](www.example.com).

    This displays the text Click on this link as a link in the published page and when you click on it, the web page www.example.com will open. You can open this in a new window or tab if you right-click on the link and select Open in a new window or Open in a new tab.

  4. Click I’m Done Editing.
Change the key benefits?
  1. Click Edit.
  2. Click in the Key benefits boxes and amend the text as required.
  3. Click I’m Done Editing.
Publish the community page?
  • Click Publish.

    • This will push any changes you’ve made to the live community page.
